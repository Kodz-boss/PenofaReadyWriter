Writings

Friday 1st March 2024

Dear Reader,

A few weeks ago I had been getting a leading in my spirit about having a worship session, I just wasn't sure if it was meant to be with people or just myself. Eventually after praying about it, I had it with my friends. For me, I've realized that occasionally I need to set myself in an atmosphere where I can just bask in the presence of God for a while and just allow His Spirit envelope me. It's perfectly okay to need that sometimes. It ignites or rather, rekindles a fire in you. Now, I Was talking to a friend and Ijust heard in my Spirit "rekindled fire" and I was a bit confused for a while so I meditated on it. I realized that the worship session we had was to rekindle a fireinside us, inside me. For a while | thought that once that fire is ignited, nothing can happen to it. It's just going to remain as bright as it should be for fire. Naturally, if you don't put out a fire it will eventually go out on its own. You can either extinguish it by pouring water or using an actual extinguisher or you can leave it for a while and watch how it disappears or you can add things that will allow the fire burn even more. Relating this to our walk with Christ, you can either let the fire that burns in you for God die by allowing the world sit in your heart, by allowing unnecessary things distract you or you can let that fire burn even brighter by constantly feeding yourselves with the word of God, praying consistently, praise and worshiping God as much as you can. (SIDE NOTE: In as much as you shouldn't underestimate the power of prayer, never ever underestimate the power of your worship and thanksgiving as well) It's very easy to let the things of the world distract you, it's so easy and tempting but my dearly beloved, do not conform to the teachings of the world. You can't do this on your own, it's easy to say "l want to burn for God" or "I want to rekindle my fire", it's very easy but you cannot do it without the help of God. A friend of mine said "Let the Holy Spirit be your 911 before your friend" and that really stuck with me. You want to burn more for God? Talk to the Holy Spirit. He's the first you should tell. God is patiently waiting for you to run to Him and talk to Him. Let Him help you rekindle that fire so you can burn more for Him. Allow yourself to be helped by God. Let your altar burn with fire! I genuinely believe that this has blessed you in more ways than one and l pray that it stirs something inside you to rekindle that fire today. I love every single one of you with my entire heart and I know for a fact, I am certain that God loves you even more'
~ From, PRW
